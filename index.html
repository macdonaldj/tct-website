US – A SHORT HISTORY

In 2009, Mike Okupinski initiated the vision for a small bottle shop and growler bar, and his buddy, Ed Buffington, joined the project shortly thereafter offering up the idea of a curated wine selection. The two firmly believed that Greenville’s culture was ready for something special. They opened The Community Tap on July 1st, 2010, to a thirsty crowd.

As the years changed over, so did the laws and the beer culture in SC. Mike and Ed could give tastes as they spoke of these great beers, later they could pour beers and wines by the glass for private events and then regularly on the sales floor. They went from 6 beer taps to 12, and were the first in the US to offer wines in a growler. The Tasting Room overflowed with customers enjoying a glass after work, families enjoying bites from the local food trucks, friends gathering on the sales floor to taste/drink and peruse the ever-growing selection hand-picked by Mike and Ed. The beer culture was ready for another change – they wanted a hang out!

April 23rd, 2014, The Community Tap moved three doors down (thanks to very thoughtful neighbors and a willing landlord!) into a larger space complete with an expanded wine and cold selection, a bar with 16 beers on tap and over 11 wines by the glass, a private event room, and a large deck with seating for 60. Anna Okupinski officially came on board (Spring 2014) as the organizer/whip-cracker for the new and improved Tap.

Ed and Mike continue to have a great time hand-picking TCT's product assortment. "There are so many great bottles out there, and a lot of them at sharp price-points – it’s an exciting time for beer and wine enthusiasts. We just want to share our passion and knowledge with others. As your neighborhood guys with local and knowledgeable staff, you’ll find approachable, with a damn good selection of beer and wine to boot."

Come have a drink with at the Tap, enjoy local food truck fare, and leave with a cold six-pack to go!

OUR SPACE

WHO CAN I INVITE? The Community Tap is kid and pooch friendly! Dogs must be lead and preferably on the deck. Please keep your children with you at all times – it is a store full of glass bottles after all! There are high chairs, games, and a small selection of craft sodas and snacks (including the ever-delicious King of Pops popsicles!) available.

WHAT ABOUT SEATING? CAN I RESERVE A TABLE? The Tap boasts indoor and (covered) outdoor seating for over 100 guests. Tables and seating is on a first-come first-dibs basis. You’re welcome to come celebrate with a group – but we do not reserve tables. For larger groups, please read information below regarding our private event room.

Y’ALL GOT FOOD? We stick to what we know and do not have a kitchen at the Tap. There is a small selection of snacks and craft sodas, and you’re welcome to bring food from home, order a pizza for delivery, or grab a bit from a local food truck, when scheduled.

PRIVATE EVENTS

The Community Tap is happy to provide a craft beer and select wine experience for your private event! We have a tasting room for onsite events, and can also come to your house or offsite venue. Whether a beer tasting for 10 or a craft beer and wine wedding experience for 200, TCT can make your event one to remember. Contacts us to check availability and book your private event!
